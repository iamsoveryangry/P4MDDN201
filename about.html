About Bored?

What the hell is this website?

I will help you waste your time better.

Heavily inspired by 'The Useless Web[?]' and 'Free Real Estate[?]', this website is to remember what we TV generation people were perpetually exposed to in the past, and also to share them with people who encountered them online and got curious what they are all about.

Infomercials are remembered in many different ways. They were annoying, they were unnecessarily long, most of the products sold seemed dodgy, actors were always playing either a very depressed character (because they never discovered the product) or an extremely exaggeratedly happy person who found the joy of life from 'As seen on TV' product.
